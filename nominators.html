Who nominated him

Every Labour MP backing Andy Burnham for leader, and their reasons in their own words where they have given them

← Back to the nominations page

322 of Labour's 403 MPs nominated Andy Burnham on the first day of nominations, 9 July 2026. LabourList's tracker, from which this list is drawn, counts 325 backing him in total: the 322 who have nominated, plus three MPs, Mike Reader, Steve Reed and Catherine West, who have publicly pledged support but not yet signed nomination papers. The remaining 78 have announced no position.

The two figures measure different things. Each MP can nominate one candidate, so the pool still able to nominate somebody else is everyone who has not yet nominated: the 78 undeclared plus the three pledgers, 81 MPs in all, exactly the 81 a challenger would need. That is why the BBC recorded the day-one tally as one short of the 323 that would make a rival mathematically impossible.

Where an MP has publicly said why they are backing him, their words appear beneath their name with a link to the source. Most MPs sign nomination papers without a public statement, so this page grows as reasons are given.
