# Inside Andy's Brain

A searchable public record of Andy Burnham: every House of Commons speech, question and division vote from his years as MP for Leigh (2001 to 2017), his ministerial speeches, his mayoral statements (2017 to 2026), curated press records on his June 2026 return to the Commons, and full-text extracts of the government reports behind his major campaigns.

12,345 records, 2001 to 2026, public sources only: Hansard via TheyWorkForYou, Public Whip division data, gov.uk and the UK Government Web Archive, Greater Manchester Combined Authority press, and extracted official reports. Every record links to its source. Press records are summary and link only for copyright reasons.

The search runs entirely in your browser. The site is static: the database ships as split chunks (`db/index.sqlite.NNN`) and the page fetches only the few kilobytes each query needs, using sql.js-httpvfs.

Built from a structured public-record vault. Data snapshot: 4 July 2026.
